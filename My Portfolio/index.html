About

Hi! I'm Sathwik M S, a 2nd-year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student at SDM College, Ujire. I have a strong interest in web development and software technology. My computer journey started when I was pursuing my early academic life in Chikkamagaluru to complete my Second PUC. In those days, it was there that I found my passion in creating digital experiences and solving real-world problems using code. The last few years, I've been venturing through different domains of software development — from crafting intuitive front-end interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to understanding the potential of back-end technologies such as Node.js and databases. Through every new project, I aim to become more capable of solving problems and closer to my aspiration of becoming a full stack web developer. I am a firm believer in ongoing learning and hands-on experience. I proactively engage in personal projects, partner with others, and do internships to learn the real world. My aim is not merely to create websites, but to design meaningful digital solutions that are efficient, responsive, and user-centric. Looking forward, I'm looking forward to collaborating with cutting-edge teams, working on meaningful projects, and continuing to break limits on what I can build as a full stack developer. Thanks for stopping by my portfolio — I hope you have as much fun browsing my work as I've had making it!
